# Core Premise & World / System Notes

## MC

- **Name:** (TBD – “MC” for outline)
    
- **Background:** Human Alliance space marine, veteran of multiple campaigns. Trained in:
    
    - Small-unit tactics, cover usage, room clearing.
        
    - Both firearm and close-quarters combat.
        
    - Mental discipline, pain management, procedure.
        

## Mana & Body

- **Foreign Mana Injection:**  
    Early on, the Dark Elf forcefully pushes mana into MC for testing.
    
    - Instead of being burned or overloaded, MC’s body **absorbs** it.
        
    - His instincts + soldier discipline let him stabilize it rather than explode.
        
- **Absorption Technique:**
    
    - The Dark Elf, intrigued, teaches him **basic mana absorption** so he doesn’t waste the experiments and doesn’t die.
        
    - MC learns to:
        
        - Draw ambient mana into his body.
            
        - Cycle it through “circuits” (meridians, veins, whatever flavor you choose).
            
        - Store it in his internal **mana pool**.
            
- **Mana Capacity:**
    
    - Total capacity is usually semi-fixed for most people; raising it is legendary-level difficulty.
        
    - The mage expects MC to cap out quickly.
        
    - Instead, MC keeps absorbing. His capacity is:
        
        - Immense.
            
        - Very slow to fill.
            
        - Deeply impressive and slightly unnerving.
            
- **Training:**
    
    - In free time and at night, MC:
        
        - Practices absorption in silence.
            
        - Does body exercises familiar from his marine training: push-ups, isometrics, balance drills, combined with mana cycling.
            
    - Result: his body and mana pool grow in sync, giving him excellent “mana-to-muscle” integration.
        

## Void / Space Magic

- **Affinity:**
    
    - MC’s affinity is **Void/Space** (very rare, poorly understood).
        
    - Most known space mages:
        
        - Specialize in **gates** (fixed teleport stations).
            
        - Use **teleportation circles** and **gate artifacts** built from rare spatial materials (space-aspected rocks, beast parts, etc.).
            
    - They focus on:
        
        - Large-scale transport.
            
        - Strategic movement.
            
        - Very little tactical innovation.
            
- **MC’s Unique Approach:**
    
    - Combines human tactical creativity + void/space.
        
    - Thinks like a soldier:
        
        - Two linked gates = sniper trick, remote stabbing, theft.
            
        - Memory-based anchoring = precise combat teleports & retreats.
            
        - Micro-gates to redirect projectiles, spells, and objects.
            
- **Artifact Context:**
    
    - Most void magic in the world is **artifact-based**:
        
        - Massive gate structures that connect cities/planets.
            
        - Portable but expensive teleport stones with limited range.
            
    - Almost nobody:
        
        - Uses pure space magic **internally**.
            
        - Uses it in **small, tactical scales**.
            
    - MC will become the first to treat void like personal weaponry, not just infrastructure.
        

## Pocket Dimension Seed

- Late in his growth (after the 10-year skip), MC:
    
    - Realizes his capacity is huge but still finite.
        
    - Conceives the idea to **create a pocket dimension inside himself**:
        
        - An inner “room” tied to his soul/body.
            
        - A dedicated **mana battery**, not for storing items (yet), but raw energy.
            
    - This is **archmage-level** spatial / soul magic.
        
    - Information in the library is sparse, theoretical, and mostly about external pocket realms.
        
    - He keeps this plan completely secret.
        
    - At this story’s level, he’s still at theory / early experiment stage—the real exploration is for future arcs.

# Episode Structure (6 Episodes)

## Episode 1 – **Broken Ship, New Master**

**Purpose:** Introduce MC, disaster, Dark Elf, language spell, and early mana absorption.

    - White-out.
        
- **Slave Market Awakening:**
    
    - MC wakes shackled in a slave pen; alien sky overhead.
        
    - Smell of sweat, blood, and spiced incense.
        
    - Various species in chains, auctioneer’s voice echoing.
        
- **Dark Elf Examination:**
    
    - Thin, elegant, silver-eyed mage in layered robes.

        - Notes strong muscle structure.
            
        - Detects unusual mana “depth.”
            
        - Intrigued by the “foreign pattern” of his body.

    - In a testing chamber, Dark Elf channels mana directly into MC:
        
        - Most slaves would convulse or burn.
            
        - MC’s instinct + training leads him to **pull it inward**.

    - Mage doesn’t want to waste a subject that can handle raw mana.

        - “Breathe in, pull mana to this center, cycle, don’t let it stagnate.”

- **Language Spell:**
    
    - Ritual room: chalk circles, the air sharp with burning herbs.
        
    - The mage presses hand to MC’s forehead, chanting.
        
    - Light and sound compress, foreign grammar and words slam into his brain.
        
    - MC collapses, bleeding from nose, now able to understand and speak broken local tongue.
        
- **Tower Arrival Cliffhanger:**
    
    - Taken to the tower, looming over strange terrain.
        
    - Inside: grimoires, glowing diagrams, caged creatures, crystals humming.
        
    - Final beat: as the Dark Elf pushes another diagnostic mana pulse through him, a scrying device briefly shows **space rippling** around MC’s body. The mage’s eyes narrow.
