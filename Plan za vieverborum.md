### **1. The Marketing Strategy: The "Cliffhanger" Funnel**

You plan to use **Video Teasers with AI Images + Voiceover** on YouTube/TikTok/IG.

- **Evaluation:** **A-**
    
- **Why it works:** Social algorithms love "visual storytelling." A static image of a text block is boring. A Ken Burns-style pan over a Cyberpunk city while a narrator reads a suspenseful Spanish sentence is engaging.
    
- **The Risk (Burnout):** producing 5 stories a week is hard. Producing 5 _videos_ a week on top of that is a full-time job.
    
- **Strict Advice:**
    
    - **The 80/20 Rule:** Do not spend hours editing. Create a **Master Template** in CapCut or Premiere. Drag and drop image, drag and drop audio, export. Total video editing time should be <20 minutes per video.
        
    - **The Hook:** The video MUST end on a cliffhanger. "The door opened, and Juan saw..." -> _Cut to black._ -> "Read the rest on [AppName]."
        

### **2. The Audio Strategy**

You have "High Quality Audio" on the site and plan to post "Audio Teasers" on audio platforms.

- **Evaluation:** **B+**
    
- **The Strength:** High-quality audio on the site is your **Retention Feature**. It prevents users from cancelling because they feel they are getting "listening practice" (which feels valuable).
    
- **The Weakness:** Posting teasers on "audio story websites" (like Soundcloud or Podcast directories) usually has **very low conversion**. Podcast listeners are passive (driving, washing dishes); they rarely click links.
    
- **Strict Advice:** Automate this. If it takes you more than 5 minutes to upload the audio teaser, skip it. Focus 90% of your energy on **Video** (YouTube/TikTok) because those users are looking at screens and can click links.
