NAME: John Silver
ROLES: protagonist
PRONOUNS: He/Him
GROUPS: Protagonist, Human, Military, Space Mage
OTHER NAMES: The Specimen" (Casil's early reference), "Void Walker" (future title)
PERSONALITY: - Surface: Calm, controlled, professional soldier demeanor

- Interior: Fiery, adventurous (reckless streak), optimistic

- Key Trait: Treats life as mission—not tragedy

- Emotional State: EXCITED by magic (not depressed about situation)

- Philosophy: "Old life is dead. This is reconnaissance of new territory."
MOTIVATIONS: - Primary: Survival through mastery

- Secondary: Understanding magic system completely

- Tertiary: Escape captivity and explore new world freely

- Deep Drive: Prove human adaptability and innovation
INTERNAL CONFLICT:     - None (resolved in Act 1)

    - Initial: Duty to family/humanity vs acceptance of new reality

    - Resolution: "I'm already dead. Ghost doesn't owe anything. Free to choose."
STRENGTHS:     - Combat: Hand-to-hand, weapons specialist (throwing knives, improvised weapons)

    - Technical: Spaceship maintenance, piloting, space navigation

    - Mental: Fast learner, pattern recognition, tactical analysis, photographic memory (military training)

    - Knowledge: Human anatomy/medicine, infiltration, espionage, terraforming basics

    - Discipline: Pain tolerance, focus endurance, emotional control

    - Innovation: Applies human military doctrine to magic (asymmetric warfare)
WEAKNESSES:   - Reckless (adventurous to fault—takes dangerous risks)

    - No social skills (military mindset, not diplomatic)

    - Overconfident in tactics (hasn't faced truly overwhelming power yet)

    - Isolated (no allies, trusts nobody)

    - Limited raw power (5th circle—vary strong for age, weak vs 7th+ circle)
CHARACTER ARC: - Prisoner (captive, doomed specimen)

    - → Secret Student (hidden advancement, survival focus)

    - → Defender (proves tactical value during attack)

    - → Apprentice (official student, preparing for betrayal)

    - → Thief (steals tower, escapes to freedom)
